About Goggaliefies

Goggaliefies Day Care Centre is a top-notch early childhood education facility that operates from Monday to Friday, from 6:30am to 5:30pm. Our experienced team of caregivers, led by Me. Larne, is dedicated to providing a well-rounded education for children aged 2 to 4 years old.

Our curriculum focuses on all areas of a child's development, including social, emotional, physical, and intellectual needs, and is taught in a nurturing small-group environment. Our play-based approach encourages children to explore, be creative, and actively participate, helping to build positive self-esteem, confidence, and independence.

Contact Us

In addition to our comprehensive curriculum, we offer optional extra-curricular activities in the morning, such as Playball for children aged 2.5 to 5 years old, to aid in their growth and development. We also host regular educational outings and "special days" to enhance the educational program. We believe in fostering a friendly and caring community, where children develop empathy and kindness towards their friends and those around them. We strive to prepare children to be responsible members of their community, equipped with the necessary social and academic skills for success in the future. Our aim is to encourage children to be confident, inquisitive, and enthusiastic about gaining more knowledge of the world around them.

At Goggaliefies Day Care Centre, traditional values and manners are emphasized, promoting children to be polite and courteous members of the school community. We also keep you informed and up-to-date through parent meetings and reports and provide opportunities for social get-togethers. Our goal is to provide a warm, nurturing environment for children to learn and grow, and to be a leader in early childhood education.

Services

Education and development for children can be enhanced through various methods such as interactive education, support, story acting, excellent educators, outdoor activities, and creative design. By engaging learners with customized hands-on experiences, fostering well-being and community through access and relationships, developing skills and emotions through imaginative storytelling, receiving expert guidance from skilled mentors, promoting development through outdoor exploration and play, and fostering self-expression and creativity through hands-on design, children can reach their full potential and grow in a positive and supportive environment.

Interactive Education

Engage learners with customized hands-on experiences.
  • Increased engagement and motivation in learning
  • Improved retention and understanding of content
  • Customized and personalized learning experiences
  • Opportunity for hands-on, practical application of concepts
  • Development of critical thinking, problem-solving and communication skills

Support

Foster well-being and community through access and relationships.
  • Emotional and mental well-being
  • Improved relationships and social connections
  • Access to resources and information
  • Enhanced resilience and coping skills
  • A sense of community and belonging

Story Acting

Develop skills and emotions through imaginative storytelling.
  • Enhanced creativity and imagination
  • Improved communication and public speaking skills
  • Development of emotional intelligence and empathy
  • Increased confidence and self-esteem
  • Opportunities for teamwork and collaboration

Excellent Educators

Receive expert guidance from skilled mentors.
  • High-quality teaching and learning experiences
  • Development of strong mentorship relationships
  • Access to expert knowledge and guidance
  • Inspiration and motivation to succeed
  • Opportunities for growth and development

Outdoor Activities

Promote development through outdoor exploration and play.
  • Physical development and improvement of gross motor skills
  • Opportunities for exploration, discovery, and hands-on learning
  • Exposure to different environments and natural elements
  • Development of social and emotional skills through play and interaction with others
  • Improved concentration, attention span and overall cognitive functioning

Creative Design

Foster self-expression and creativity through hands-on design.
  • Development of fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination
  • Encouragement of creativity, imagination, and self-expression
  • Opportunities for problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Introduction to different materials, textures, and colors
  • Building of self-esteem and confidence through self-expression
